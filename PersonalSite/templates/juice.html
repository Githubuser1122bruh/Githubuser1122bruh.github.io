Juice is a very interesting story, and what better way to showcase that story than an arranged list of what happend on each day!

So, here is the list of events that happened on each day:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Shanghai. Pretty much everyone made it to Shanghai and jetlag was killing people, we had everyone checked in to the hotel, and a journey awaiting every single person there!
  • Day 2: Kickoff, we made it to the first venue, Bottle Dream, it was a pretty nice place and everyone in the room had a lot of hope and excitement towards the event. Thomas explained the ground rules on the trip, the premise, and a base outline of what we were going to be doing on our trip.
  • Day 3: People walking into the cafe, and us figuring out how the juicer worked. We ran into a problem, we had no clue how this weird machine that they had at Bottle Dream even worked...
  • Day 4: Team-building activities and first real customers (according to Thomas) and first team events...
  • Day 5: We moved to a new venue (my team being the first to actually see it, it was awesome)...
  • Day 6: This day was similar to Day 3, but now that we had the juicer figured out, everything went much smoother...
  • Day 7: The final day for most people. Everyone started leaving, and the venue felt quieter as the day went on...
  • Day 8: This was the day I left. Since I stayed an extra day, I had some time to reflect on the entire experience...

And that is the story of how 90 teenagers went to Shanghai for a week.
